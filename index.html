learn and explore

live-video analysis

Live video analysis in mental health therapy uses emotions and sentiments detection to provide deeper insights into a patient’s emotional state. This approach enhances remote sessions, allowing therapists to deliver more personalized and effective care.

recorded video analysis

Recorded video analysis in mental health therapy enables therapists to review and assess a patient's emotional state over time using advanced emotion detection technology. This method offers valuable insights, allowing for more informed and tailored treatment plans.

audio based analysis

Audio-based analysis in mental health therapy uses voice tone, pitch, and sentiment analysis to gauge a patient's emotional state. This technology enhances therapy by providing deeper insights into emotions through speech patterns.

what Researchers says about quantifying emotions

Researchers and practitioners rely on emotion quantification techniques to gain a deeper understanding of the efficacy of cognitive reappraisal. By quantifying emotions, the researchers can explore the impact of cognitive reappraisal on emotion regulation and its potential therapeutic benefits
